T25. Species as Values

A species can be made a value and treated like a data item. Currently, the capabilities are very rudimentary, but they will be improved later. The currently available features are as follows.

ASpecies
When a species is treated as a value, its species is ASpecies.

speciesAsValue
Expression speciesAsValue T is species T, as a value of species ASpecies.

Example: speciesAsValue (Integer,Integer) is species (Integer,Integer), as a value.

speciesOf
speciesOf(x) is the (extrinsic) species of x, as a value of species ASpecies.

Example: speciesOf("abc") = [Char].

intrinsicSpeciesOf
intrinsicSpeciesOf(x) the intrinsic species of x, as a value of species ASpecies.

Example: intrinsicSpeciesOf(<<"abc">>:ANY) = [Char].

$
$(t) is a string that describes t.

Example: $(speciesAsValue (Integer,Integer)) = "(Integer,Integer)".
