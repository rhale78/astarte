LN4. Secondary Numeric Species

For each numeric genus (such as REAL and RFIELD) there is a secondary species. Operations on numbers with secondary species work by converting numbers to a common primary species. For example, 
  <<2:Natural>> + <<5:Rational>> = <<7:Rational>>
These operations use compatify to force their operands to have a common intrinsic species.
